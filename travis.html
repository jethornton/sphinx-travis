Travis-CI¶

Go to travis-ci.org and sign in with GitHub.

Click the + sign next to My Repositories to view all your repositories.

If you don’t see your repository click on Sync account.

Go to Settings and in Environment Variables put GH_TOKEN in the name field and the personal token in the Value field. Click Add. Click on Current tab and click Activate respository.

Now when you upload it should trigger a build of the documents.

Once you have a sucessful build your documents will be at https://username.github.io/repo-name
